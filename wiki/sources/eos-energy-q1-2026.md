# Eos Energy Q1 2026: +445% Revenue, Cerberus Partnership (May 2026)

> Source: https://www.aol.com/finance/why-eos-energy-stock-jumped-190937498.html
> Published: 2026-05-14 (Motley Fool via AOL Finance)

## Summary

Eos Energy (NASDAQ: EOSE) reported Q1 2026 revenue of $57M (+445% YoY) on 5.7x more battery "cubes" delivered plus factory automation gains. More importantly, the company announced a partnership with Cerberus Capital to create Frontier Power USA — a joint venture to build and operate long-duration energy storage projects using Eos's zinc battery technology, with $100M committed and a 2 GWh supply agreement. The bankability milestone is more significant than the revenue number. Stock surged ~23% on open, settled ~+10%.

## Key Claims

- **Q1 revenue**: $57M, +445% YoY; 5.7x more battery cubes delivered; factory automation improvements
- **EPS**: +$0.12 vs -$0.20 year ago — driven by non-operating items; gross/operating loss widened as costs rose with scale
- **Cerberus deal**: Frontier Power USA JV; $100M committed; Eos signs 2 GWh supply agreement — "bankability" milestone
- **Full-year guidance**: $300–400M revenue reaffirmed
- **Backlog**: $644.6M (2.6 GWh capacity)
- **Commercial pipeline**: $24.3B
- **Technology**: Zinc-based BESS chemistry; long-duration energy storage (LDES)
