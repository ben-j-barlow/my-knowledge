# Buy TSMC: BofA Reiterates on Technology Symposium (May 2026)

> Source: https://uk.finance.yahoo.com/news/buy-tsmc-stock-recent-concerns-152813087.html
> Author: Sam Boughedda (Investing.com)
> Published: 2026-05-15

## Summary

BofA analyst Haas Liu reiterated a Buy on TSMC with a NT$2,560 target following TSMC's May 2026 technology symposium. The note argues that competitive concerns around TSMC's foundry dominance are overdone — its scale and technological lead at advanced nodes continue to widen the gap with Samsung and Intel.

## Key Claims

- **3nm capacity**: 25% CAGR 2022–2027; targeting 190,000 WPM by Q4 2026, rising to 230,000 by 2027
- **Competitors at 3nm**: Samsung SF3 and Intel 18A each at only 20,000–25,000 WPM at low yields, prioritized for internal use — limits risk of Apple M-series defection from TSMC
- **N2 node**: 70% CAGR capacity growth 2026–2028; 5 fabrication facilities rolling out simultaneously; 20% faster technology transfer times; defect density target reached **2 quarters ahead** of schedule set by 3nm ramp
- **CoWoS advanced packaging**: yield already >98%; capacity growing 80% CAGR through 2027
- **SoIC (3D stacking)**: capacity growing at 90% CAGR through 2027
- **Intel EMIB-T** (competing CoWoS alternative): 80–85% pilot yield; must reach 95% for mass production by mid-2027 — execution risk flagged
