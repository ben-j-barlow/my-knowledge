# Tesla Optimus: Piper Sandler SOTP Shows Optimus "Free" at $400/Share (May 2026)

> Source: https://finance.yahoo.com/markets/stocks/articles/elon-musk-sees-15-trillion-193109583.html
> Author: Chris Katje (Benzinga)
> Published: 2026-05-16

## Summary

Piper Sandler analyst Alexander Potter maintained an Overweight rating on Tesla with a $500 price target. His sum-of-the-parts model assigns ~$400/share to 17 non-Optimus product lines (including separate line items for FSD, robotaxi, and insurance not in prior models). Optimus is excluded entirely — meaning at current prices investors get the humanoid robot program for free. Musk has said 80% of Tesla's eventual value will come from Optimus.

## Key Claims

- **SOTP**: 17 Tesla product lines = ~$400/share; Optimus excluded and treated as free optionality above $400
- **New model additions**: Insurance and robotaxi now modeled separately from FSD (not in prior Piper Sandler SOTP)
- **Musk's view**: "80% of Tesla's value will be Optimus" — contingent on execution of autonomous transport and Optimus
- **Potter**: "Optimus and inference-as-a-service are thesis-defining products that, arguably, will be worth more than Tesla's other businesses combined"
- **California $1B commercial EV rebate**: $250M initial funding starting June 26, 2026; $7,500–$120,000 rebates on new electric medium/heavy-duty commercial vehicles through 2030 via LCFS
