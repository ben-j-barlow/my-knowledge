# SK Hynix ATH: Big Tech Offering to Co-Finance HBM Fabs (May 2026)

> Source: https://www.newscase.com/sk-hynix-shares-hit-all-time-high-as-tech-giants-offer-to-co-finance-new-chip-plants/
> Published: 2026-05-13

## Summary

Microsoft, Google, and Amazon have approached SK Hynix with proposals to co-finance new ASML EUV lithography machines (~$400M each) in exchange for guaranteed HBM supply. The signal: supply is so constrained that hyperscalers are willing to bankroll their own suppliers' capacity expansion. SK Hynix shares hit a record 1,976,000 won (+8%). Q1 operating margin of 72%.

## Key Claims

- **Co-financing offers**: Microsoft, Google, Amazon each proposing to share ASML EUV machine costs (~$400M each) for guaranteed supply access
- **M15X facility** (Cheongju): purpose-built for HBM4; timeline pulled forward 2 months; target 70,000 WPM by end 2026; full mass production Q3 2026
- **Q1 financials**: Revenue +198% to 50T+ won; operating margin 72%
- **Analyst targets**: UBS raised to 1.7M won; Daishin Securities: 2.5M won
- **Goldman Sachs**: supply gap "most severe in 15 years," could persist to 2030
- **KOSPI concentration**: SK Hynix + Samsung = 42%+ of entire KOSPI market cap
- **Dividend**: 375 won/share, ex-date May 28, 2026
- **Microsoft meeting**: SK Hynix leadership met Satya Nadella and Bill Gates at Microsoft HQ regarding HBM3E supply for Maia 200 accelerator
- **Management caution**: Wary of over-dependence on few buyers and loss of pricing flexibility
