# Rocket Lab Q1 2026: Revenue +63%, Neutron Nears First Flight (May 2026)

> Source: https://24newslatest.com/post/rocket-labs-q1-surge-revenue-jumps-63-as-neutron-nears-first-flight-and-backlog-hits-record-highs
> Author: Sophia Reynolds
> Published: 2026-05-13

## Summary

Rocket Lab delivered a strong Q1 2026 beat. Revenue hit $200.3M (+63.5% YoY) with Q2 guidance of $232.5M midpoint — 12% above analyst consensus. Record backlog, record cash, and record launch contracts signed. CEO Peter Beck positioned the company as a "vertically integrated space systems" platform, not just a launch provider.

## Key Claims

- **Q1 revenue**: $200.3M, +63.5% YoY
- **Q2 guidance**: $232.5M midpoint; 12% above analyst consensus
- **Non-GAAP EPS**: -$0.02 (beat by $0.02)
- **Records**: Backlog, cash position, and launch contracts signed — all at records
- **Neutron**: Medium-lift rocket (~13,000 kg) nearing first flight; positioned as SpaceX medium-lift competitor
- **Acquisitions**: Motive Space Systems (spacecraft systems); Mynaric (laser communications for satellite subsystems)
- **Capex**: Elevated as company ramps testing and production infrastructure ahead of Neutron first flight
- **Stock**: $99.53 post-earnings, up from $78.70 pre-earnings; doubled vs one year prior
- **Bear case**: Still posting losses; priced at a premium implying near-flawless execution
