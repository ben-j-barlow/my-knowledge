# HANMI Semiconductor: Hanmi USA Established in San Jose (May 2026)

> Source: https://www.mk.co.kr/en/business/12049224
> Author: Park Sora
> Published: 2026-05-15

## Summary

HANMI Semiconductor is establishing Hanmi USA in San Jose, CA to service growing demand from new US semiconductor production facilities. HANMI holds #1 global market share in TC bonders — the critical equipment for HBM die stacking. Chairman Kwak Dong-shin: TC bonder orders are concentrating in Q2 2026 as HBM4 mass production gains momentum, with acceleration expected in H2.

## Key Claims

- **Hanmi USA**: Local subsidiary opening San Jose by end of 2026 with local engineers and technical support
- **TC bonders**: #1 global market share; orders concentrating Q2 2026 due to HBM4 ramp
- **Products**: 2.5D TC Bonder 40 and 120 (for foundry and OSAT); BOC COB Bonder (began shipping to global memory companies in 2026)
- **Next-gen**: Second-generation hybrid bonder prototype expected H2 2026
- **Aerospace**: EMI Shield 2.0 X series; also #1 market share in that segment
- Chairman: "As mass production of HBM4 gains momentum this year, TC bonder orders are concentrating in Q2, and this trend will accelerate in H2"
