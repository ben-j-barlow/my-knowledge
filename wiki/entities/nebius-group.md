# Nebius Group (NASDAQ: NBIS)

US-listed AI cloud infrastructure company. Builds and operates GPU-based AI compute data centers. Formerly part of the Yandex ecosystem. As of May 2026, one of the fastest-growing AI cloud stocks on public markets.

## Financials (Q1 2026)

- AI cloud revenue: +841% YoY (off a low base)
- Total revenue: +684% YoY
- Contracted data center power capacity: guidance raised to 4 GW+ for 2026 (up 1/3 vs prior)
- Net losses: widened vs year ago

## Infrastructure

- 4 GW+ contracted data center power capacity for 2026
- Broke ground on first US gigawatt-scale AI factory in Q2 2026
- Gigawatt-scale GPU cluster approach, not shared multi-tenant cloud
