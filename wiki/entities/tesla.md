# Tesla (NASDAQ: TSLA)

US company spanning EVs, autonomous driving (FSD/robotaxi), energy storage (Powerwall/Megapack), and humanoid robotics (Optimus). CEO: Elon Musk. The investment thesis has shifted substantially from EV manufacturer toward a multi-segment platform where Optimus and autonomous transport are the long-term value drivers.

## Business Segments (Piper Sandler SOTP model, May 2026)

Analyst Alexander Potter (Piper Sandler) models 17 product lines summing to ~$400/share:
- Automotive (EV sales)
- Full Self-Driving (FSD) subscriptions
- Robotaxi operations (now modeled separately from FSD)
- Energy storage
- Insurance (newly separated line item)
- Tesla Semi / commercial
- Service and charging network

## Optimus

Tesla's humanoid robot program. Excluded from Potter's financial model — treated as free optionality above $400/share.

- Musk: "80% of Tesla's value will be Optimus" if autonomous transport and Optimus execution succeeds
- Potter: "Optimus and inference-as-a-service are thesis-defining products that, arguably, will be worth more than Tesla's other businesses combined"

## Analyst View (May 2026)

- **Piper Sandler** (Alexander Potter): Overweight, PT $500

## Context: California Commercial EV Rebate

California announced a $1B EV rebate program for commercial vehicles starting June 26, 2026 ($250M initial, $1B+ through 2030 via LCFS). Rebates $7,500–$120,000 on medium/heavy-duty electric vehicles. Relevant for Tesla Semi.
