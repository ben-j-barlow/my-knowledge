- Valuation: ~72x expected 2026 sales — high-risk, high-multiple momentum stock

Stock gained +5.5% for the week, +16% YTD, on sector momentum from Quantum Computing Inc.'s strong Q1 results (Quantum Computing acquired Luminar Semiconductor, a photonics company guiding $20–25M revenue for 2026).
