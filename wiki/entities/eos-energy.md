# Eos Energy Enterprises (NASDAQ: EOSE)

US battery energy storage systems (BESS) company. Uses zinc-based chemistry ("Znyth") rather than standard lithium-ion — suited for long-duration storage (LDES) and removes lithium supply chain exposure. As of Q1 2026: rapid revenue ramp but still operating at a gross/operating loss.

## Technology

Zinc-based BESS. Battery "cubes" are the core modular delivery unit. Long-duration energy storage focus (4–12+ hours, vs. 2–4 hours for typical lithium-ion BESS).

## Q1 2026 Financials

- Revenue: $57M (+445% YoY)
- Cubes delivered: 5.7x prior year
- EPS: +$0.12 vs -$0.20 year ago — driven by non-operating items; gross/operating losses widened

## Key Partnership: Frontier Power USA (May 2026)

Joint venture with Cerberus Capital Management:
- Cerberus commits $100M
- Frontier Power USA builds and operates long-duration energy storage projects using Eos technology
- Eos signs 2 GWh supply agreement

Significance: First institutional commitment to Eos project financing — the "bankability" milestone critics had questioned.

## Guidance and Pipeline (2026)

- Full-year revenue guidance: $300–400M
- Backlog: $644.6M (2.6 GWh capacity)
- Commercial pipeline: $24.3B
