# HANMI Semiconductor

South Korean semiconductor equipment company. Holds the #1 global market share in TC bonders — the thermocompression bonding equipment critical for stacking HBM dies. Also holds #1 market share in EMI shielding equipment for aerospace. Chairman: Kwak Dong-shin.

## Core Products

| Product | Market | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|
| TC Bonder | HBM memory production | #1 global market share; orders concentrating Q2 2026 with HBM4 ramp |
| 2.5D TC Bonder 40 | Foundry and OSAT | AI semiconductor packaging |
| 2.5D TC Bonder 120 | Foundry and OSAT | Higher throughput variant |
| BOC COB Bonder | Global memory companies | Began shipping 2026 |
| Gen 2 hybrid bonder | TBD | Prototype expected H2 2026 |
| EMI Shield 2.0 X | Aerospace | #1 global market share |

## US Expansion

- Establishing **Hanmi USA** in San Jose, CA by end of 2026
- Local engineers and technical support to serve new US semiconductor production facilities
- Driven by Samsung, SK Hynix, TSMC, Intel US fab investments

## Investment Thesis

Pick-and-shovel HBM exposure. TC bonder demand is directly proportional to HBM output volume — every HBM chip shipped requires TC bonding equipment. As HBM4 and HBM4E ramp globally, TC bonder order volume follows.
