# SK Hynix

South Korean memory semiconductor company. World's largest supplier of high-bandwidth memory (HBM) — the stacked DRAM that sits next to the GPU on AI accelerators. As of May 2026, holds ~57% of the global HBM market and ~70% of Nvidia's HBM orders.

## Market Position (May 2026)

- **Market cap**: ~$948B (May 14, 2026); up 9x in under 2 years; +200% YTD
- **HBM market share**: ~57% overall; ~70% of Nvidia HBM orders (Counterpoint Research)
- **2026 HBM supply**: Confirmed sold out by CFO in October 2025; shortages expected into 2027
- SK Hynix + Samsung account for 42%+ of the entire KOSPI market cap

## HBM Product Roadmap

| Generation | Status (May 2026) |
|-----------|-------------------|
| HBM3E | In production; supplied for Nvidia Hopper/Blackwell, Microsoft Maia 200 |
| HBM4 | Mass production started February 2026; expected to overtake HBM3E as revenue share from Q3 2026 |
| HBM4E | Sample shipments H2 2026; mass production targeted 2027 |

Supplies ~2/3 of Nvidia's HBM4 demand for the Vera Rubin platform.

## Financials (Q1 2026)

- Revenue: 50T+ won (first time); nearly tripled YoY
- Operating margin: 72%
- R&D: 2.55T won ($1.71B), +68.3% YoY
- Capex: 7.35T won ($4.93B), +21.9% YoY
- Dividend: 375 won/share (ex-date May 28, 2026)

## Infrastructure

- **M15X facility** (Cheongju): purpose-built for HBM4; pulled forward 2 months; target 70k WPM by end 2026; full mass production Q3 2026; total cost 19T won ($12.85B)

## Hyperscaler Co-Financing

Microsoft, Google, and Amazon have approached SK Hynix offering to co-finance ASML EUV machines (~$400M each) for guaranteed supply access. Management cautious about over-dependence and pricing flexibility risk. SK Hynix leadership met Satya Nadella and Bill Gates at Microsoft HQ re: HBM3E supply for Maia 200.
