## detail

Establishing a local subsidiary in San Jose to respond to AI packaging demand, expected to expand HBM4 orders and strengthen its push into the TC bonder market

![Kwak Dong-shin, chairman of HANMI Semiconductor Co., Ltd.](https://wimg.mk.co.kr/news/cms/202605/15/news-p.v1.20260515.bdc5f5680c4349fdb9ea33d260136c4d_P1.jpg)

Kwak Dong-shin, chairman of HANMI Semiconductor Co., Ltd.

HANMI Semiconductor Co., Ltd. is moving into the U.S. market in earnest. The company plans to establish a local subsidiary and strengthen its global customer response system in line with growing demand for AI semiconductors.

HANMI Semiconductor Co., Ltd. said on the 15th that it will establish a local subsidiary, Hanmi USA, in San Jose, California, by the end of this year. The San Jose office will serve as an integrated hub for the U.S. market, with local engineers and technical support teams in place to speed up customer response.

Kwak Dong-shin, chairman of HANMI Semiconductor Co., Ltd., said, "As mass production of HBM4 gains momentum this year, TC bonder orders are concentrating in the second quarter, and this trend will accelerate in the second half." He added, "HANMI Semiconductor Co., Ltd.'s TC bonder, which holds the No. 1 global market share, will benefit even more as the AI semiconductor market expands."

HANMI Semiconductor Co., Ltd. plans to strengthen its local support system as new semiconductor production facilities expand in the United States.

HANMI Semiconductor Co., Ltd. continues to hold the No. 1 global market share in TC bonders, a key piece of equipment for HBM production. The company also plans to unveil a prototype of its next-generation second-generation hybrid bonder later this year.

It also plans to supply its AI semiconductor packaging equipment, the "Hanmi Semiconductor 2.5D TC Bonder 40" and "Hanmi Semiconductor 2.5D TC Bonder 120," to foundry and OSAT companies. Its "BOC COB Bonder," unveiled earlier this year, has also begun shipments to global memory companies.

In the aerospace sector, the company is supplying its "EMI Shield 2.0 X" series to global customers. HANMI Semiconductor Co., Ltd. said it also maintains the No. 1 market share in that equipment segment.
